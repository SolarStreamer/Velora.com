About

Velora is a next-generation automotive company built on a simple but powerful promise: Clean speed for a cleaner planet. We design sleek, instantly recognizable vehicles that deliver high performance without compromising environmental responsibility. Our cars are engineered for everyone who values speed, style, and sustainability. Whether you're chasing adrenaline or cruising in comfort, Velora adapts to your lifestyle with three signature driving modes: - Comfort Mode: Reclining seats and a smooth ride for everyday ease - xComfort Mode: Enhanced suspension and rear-seat entertainment for elevated luxury - Sports Mode: Raw power and precision—our goal is to place at least three Velora models among the fastest production cars in the world Velora is more than a brand. It’s a movement toward accessible eco-performance. We believe the future of driving should be fast, clean, and unforgettable. Velora—keep the planet clean, for less.
